Chhole Bhature

Preparation Time: 60 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (chhole)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Chhole masala
  • Flour (maida)
  • Curd
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight and boil until soft
  2. Prepare gravy with onion, tomato, and spices
  3. Add boiled chickpeas and cook well
  4. Knead dough using maida, curd, and baking soda
  5. Let the dough rest for 2-3 hours
  6. Roll and deep fry bhature until golden
  7. Serve hot with chhole

Chef Tips

For fluffy bhature, let the dough rest properly and fry in hot oil.

Nutrition

Calories: 450 kcal

Serving

Serve hot with onions, pickle, and lemon wedges.
