Paneer Roll

Preparation Time: 35 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • Wheat flour or maida (for roti/paratha)
  • Onion (sliced)
  • Capsicum
  • Tomato sauce or green chutney
  • Spices (salt, red chili powder, garam masala)
  • Oil or butter

Steps

  1. Knead dough and make roti or paratha
  2. Cut paneer into strips
  3. Sauté onion and capsicum in oil
  4. Add paneer and spices, cook for few minutes
  5. Spread sauce or chutney on roti
  6. Place paneer mixture and roll tightly
  7. Serve hot

Chef Tips

Use fresh paneer and add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra flavor.

Nutrition

Calories: 300 kcal

Serving

Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
