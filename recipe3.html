Rasmalai

Preparation Time: 45 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Lemon juice (for curdling milk)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands
  • Pistachios (chopped)

Steps

  1. Boil milk and add lemon juice to make chenna (paneer)
  2. Knead chenna and make small balls
  3. Cook balls in sugar syrup
  4. Boil milk separately with sugar, saffron, and cardamom
  5. Add cooked balls into thickened milk
  6. Garnish with pistachios and chill

Chef Tips

Use full cream milk for soft rasmalai and always cook on low flame.

Nutrition

Calories: 300 kcal

Serving

Serve chilled for best taste.
