White Sauce Pasta

Preparation Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Maida (flour)
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta and keep aside
  2. Heat butter and sauté garlic
  3. Add maida and cook for a minute
  4. Slowly add milk and stir to avoid lumps
  5. Add salt, pepper, and cheese
  6. Mix boiled pasta into the sauce
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot

Chef Tips

Keep stirring while adding milk to get a smooth and creamy sauce.

Nutrition

Calories: 350 kcal

Serving

Serve hot with garlic bread or toasted bread.
